Our Journey Begins

Koe Mau memulai perjalanannya pada tahun 2021 sebagai usaha rumahan yang menyajikan dimsum sebagai cemilan keluarga. Dengan berbagai varian topping premium seperti ayam pilihan, jamur shitake, wortel segar, daging sapi berkualitas, dan sosis premium, produk kami dipromosikan melalui penyebaran brosur ke jaringan teman, kerabat, serta mitra cafe dan warung kopi terdekat.

Inspirasi bisnis ini muncul dari tingginya permintaan akan cita rasa autentik dari dimsum. Dengan menjangkau lingkungan perkantoran dan melalui strategi promosi yang efektif, Dimsum KoeMau semakin dikenal dan diminati oleh berbagai kalangan.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To create uniquely flavorful KoeMau Frozen Dimsum that captivates taste buds and leaves a lasting impression, making every bite an unforgettable experience.

Vision: To innovate and diversify our flavor profiles, continuously delighting our customers while expanding the reach of KoeMau Dimsum through a robust network of sellers and resellers.

Our Core Principles

At KoeMau, our foundation rests on unwavering principles:

  • Consistent Quality: We are dedicated to maintaining the highest product quality and a consistent, authentic taste in every batch.
  • Halal & Nutritious Ingredients: We meticulously select halal, healthy, and nutritious ingredients, ensuring peace of mind for our customers.
  • Premium Packaging: We pay close attention to packaging quality, guaranteeing freshness and convenience from our kitchen to your table.

Commitment to Excellence

Our commitment to culinary excellence is paramount. We proudly use fresh, high-quality raw ingredients and adhere to a meticulous manual production process. The natural, rich flavors of our dimsum are a testament to our use of authentic spices and the complete absence of artificial preservatives.
